The objective of the Student Management System(SMS)- Digital Empowerment to the Students in keeping view of Digital India. SMS provides all services of the Institutions Online to the Students. Advantage of SMS to easily track records like Students Database Management, Scholar Register Management, Enrollment on any time from any where.

A Student Management System is also known as a Student Information System (SIS). These systems work to coordinate scheduling and communications between faculty regarding students. This system exists to simplify information tracking for both parents and administrative staff. SMS also keeps track on administrative staff of college.
